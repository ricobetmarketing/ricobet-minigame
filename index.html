How to subscribe (so updates & notifications work)

Apple (iPhone / iPad / Mac)
Tap the yellow “Subscribe” button in Safari → Accept → Done. Settings ▸ Calendar ▸ Accounts ▸ Subscribed Calendars to manage.
Google Calendar (Desktop)
Click “Add to Google” → Calendar opens with the feed URL → Add calendar. Updates may take up to 24h.
Outlook / Android apps
Outlook Web: Calendar ▸ Add calendar ▸ Subscribe from web. Android: Use an app like ICSx⁵ to subscribe for near real-time updates.

Subscribed calendars stay live — when we add, change, or delete events, your calendar updates automatically.
